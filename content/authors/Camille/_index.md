I am a recent graduate of UC Davis with a degree in Marine and Coastal sciences. I am broadly interested in genomics, adaptation, and plasticity. I am particularly interested in how organisms adapt to natural environmental gradients, as well as how human activities impact this adaptation. I am hoping to pursue a PhD in 2022, and am excited to be working with the Bay Lab until then. I also work part-time for Eco-Alpha as an environmental technician, and in my spare time, you can find me SCUBA diving, rollerblading, or making tiny hats for my lizard.
